How to Implement

The attributes that accordion supports are as follows:

  • clickable_heading: 'Heading that you wish to click on for the accordion.'
  • content_div: 'content div that follows each heading tag you specify.'
	$('body').accordion({

		clickable_heading: 'h2',

		content_div: '.accordion_content'

	});
